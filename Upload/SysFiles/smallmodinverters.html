Designed to power electronics and small appliances on the go, the Small Modified Inverters converts 12.8-volt DC battery power from a vehicle into 115-Volt AC househould electricity. Providing a convenient and powerful means of accessing AC power, the Small Modified Inverters are available in a range of sizes from 75 to 500 Watts. These inverters can power a variety of electronic products including mobile phones, tablets, portable DVD players, laptops, portable printers, small power tools, and work lights.
