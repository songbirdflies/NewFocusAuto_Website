Innovation. It’s the governing principle that has transform New Focus Lighting & Power Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd (NFLP) from a small digital inverter manufacturer to becoming a global supplier of high quality automotive products. From sophisticated automotive electronics to everyday car hold accessories, our diverse range of products have bring convenience and satisfaction to millions of customers worldwide.

Since its inception in 1994, NFLP designs, develops and manufactures a series of user friendly products for the auto, marine, outdoor and industrial markets. Our diverse products are separated into five categories: 1. Power inverters 2. High Frequency Smart Charger 3. Multipurpose Powerpack and Jumpstarts 4. Automotive and outdoor HID lights. 5. Cooler and Warmer boxes.

Under the company’s “Best Product, Best Service, Best Value” business model, NFLP invested heavily in R&D and incorporate new ideas and technologies in improving our products and make them smaller, lighter, safer and smarter for our customers. All of our products have all been certified by UL, ETL, CE, E-MARK, TUV, FCC, SAE, CETL, C-TICK and GS.

Through our close collaboration with renowned institutes such as Fudan University, Shanghai JiaoTong University and Hwadong Institute of Science and Technology, NFLP has set an industry record on research and development. Over the years, NFLP has applied for 358 patents for our products, 9 of which are international patents and 327 of which have been patented by the Chinese government. For these remarkable achievements, NFLP has gain national recogntions from both federal government and local municipal government, receiving honors such as “China’s Top Ten Patent Enterprise” “China’s Top Ten Trademark Enterprise” and “Model Unit of Patent application.” NFLP products have been selected and applied in the Shanghai Hi-Tech Program and Shanghai Torch Program by Shanghai Science and Technology Committee.

Our state of the art manufacturing facility located in Shanghai Qingpu Industrial Park gives NFLP control over production times, efficency and quality. The plant is designed and constructed to meet the ISO 9000 family of Quality Standards and have achieved certifications of ISO9001, ISO9002 QS9000 and ISO/TS16949 certification in 2004.

As China’s domestic automotive slowly matures, NFLP has also start to expand into the vast local Chinese market. First with the creation of an indigenous brand, “NFA” and later with the creation of AutoLife, an automotive accessories retailers. These initiatives allows NFLP to establish and introduce our products through these extensive distributing channels.

On February 28th, 2005, New Focus Auto Tech Holding went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange with the stock number HK0360. This is the first time for an automotive aftermarket retailers in mainland China to be listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.
