Global Vision  From sophisticated automotive electronics to everyday battery terminal accessories, our diverse ranges of products has brought convenient and satisfying autolife to millions of customers worldwide.

Innovation  Under the company’s “Best Product, Best Service, Best Value” policy, NFA invests heavily in research and development, incorporating new ideas and technologies in improving our products and dedicating to make them smaller, lighter, safer, smarter and more fashionable for our customers.

NFA closely collaborates with renowned institutes such as Fudan University, Shanghai JiaoTong University and Huadong Institute of Science and Technology.

NFA has set an industry record on research and development. Over the years, NFA has applied for 358 patents for our products both internationally and with the Chinese government. For these remarkable achievements, NFA has gained national recognitions from federal government and local municipal government agencies, receiving honors such as “China’s Top Ten Patent Enterprise” “China’s Top Ten Trademark Enterprise” and “Model Unit of Patent application.” NFA products have been selected and applied in the Shanghai Hi-Tech Program and Shanghai Torch Program by Shanghai Science and Technology Committee.

High Quality  NFA products meet the highest international standards for premium automotive part and accessory products, all of our products have all been certificated with: UL, ETL, CE, E-MARK, TUV, FCC, SAE, CETL, C-TICK and GS. Our state of the art manufacturing facility located in Shanghai Qingpu Industrial Park gives NFA control over production times, efficiency and quality. The plant was designed and constructed to meet the ISO 9000 family of Quality Standards and has achieved certifications of ISO9001, ISO9002 QS9000 and ISO/TS16949 certification in 2004. 

Localization  NFA Started to expand into the vast local Chinese market since 2001 by the creation of Autolife - an automotive service retailer. With a decade development, NFA currently owns 354 chained franchise stores nationwide, which allows us to offer our high quality products and serve customers more efficiently and extensively.
On February 28th, 2005, NFA went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange with the stock number HK0360. This is the first time for an automotive aftermarket retailer in mainland China to be listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

NFA Products Family  NFA designs, develops and manufactures a complete line of user-friendly products for the automotive, marine, outdoor and industrial markets. Our diverse products are separated into SIX categories:
1, NFA DC to AC Power Inverters;
2, NFA Power Packs & Jump Starters;
3, 12 Volt Battery Chargers;
4, NFA Cooler andvWarmer Box;
5, NFA HID Off-road & Work Lights;
6, NFA Booster Cables.
Our products have been exported to USA, Canada, Europe, Japan and many countries in South East Asia.
