Employee Development Strategy
NFA regards human resource as the first source of enterprise, providing room for the right employee who can grow within the company, encourage potential and creativity through competition, and measure employee performance through their ability and contribution.


Better Staff And Better Company
NFA promotes the employee’s career development through establishing a learning team by providing vast training opportunities. The training includes domestic and international freshman training, management training and overseas expeditions, in order to advance the employee’s abilities and qualifications. Not only will this improve on the employee’s individual value, but it will also continually update NFA’s knowledge and competitiveness.

 

Manpower's policy
NFA insists on the conception of "human-oriented, consumer paramount, scientific management, quality is the first thing", we regard talents as the enterprise's basis of competition and development. The company has established an environment of hard working, fair play, respect knowledge and talent. We have a talent recruitment strategy of open career development, good culture environment, and competitive compensation, which makes the company become a talent centralizer's stage and staff more excellent.
