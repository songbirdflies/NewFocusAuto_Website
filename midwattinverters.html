Ideal for powering recreational, mobile office equipment, and other electronic appliences, the Small Modified Inverters convert 12 Volts of DC battery power in your vehicle to 110 Volts of AC power. Available in a range of sizes from 120 to 400 Watts. The inverters can power a variety of electronic products including mobile phones, tablets, portable DVD players, laptops, camcorders, stereos, small power tools, and work lights.
