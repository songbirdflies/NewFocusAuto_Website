New Focus Auto and Michelin

New Focus Auto enters into 3 year licensing agreement with Michelin for distribution of MICHELIN branded auto accessory products in Mainland China

New Focus Auto Tech Holdings Ltd. (HKEX 0360, TWSE 9106) announced the completion of an licensing agreement with the world leading tire manufacturer, Michelin, for the manufacturing and distribution of MICHELIN branded auto accessory products in mainland China.  The agreement is a 3-year contract whereby New Focus Lighting & Power Technology (Shanghai) Co.,Ltd, a fully-owned subsidiary of NFA Group will manufacture MICHELIN  products- a series of auto related consumer accessories including car inverters, coolers and warmers, 4 in 1 multi-purpose emergency jumpstarter, series of LED work lights, booster cables, car beauty kits, etc.- under license.

In this undertaking, NFA will be responsible for product development, promotional planning, sales, distribution and after-sales services to Chinese customers.  .  All products will be distributed through   NFA’s 100+ direct-owned service retail chains, its cross-region wholesale channels throughout Greater China and also through other retail stores.

"Joining forces with Michelin creates a compelling new market proposition and choice for Chinese car owners,” said Raymond Chang, the Chief Executive Officer of NFA Group. "Given the strong brand awareness of Michelin in automobile tires and accessories, we are confident that we will identify, develop, and manufacture the most compelling and attractive auto-accessory products for the fast growing China auto market.  This strategic cooperation will bring about a win-win partnership for Michelin and NFA."

NFA is delivering on an aggressive growth strategy. “It is truly exciting to be named a major product developer and manufacturer for China’s fastest-growing domestic auto company,” said Ted Leung, General Manager of New Focus Lighting & Power Technology (Shanghai) Co.,Ltd. “Our

 job now is to execute our plans and continue the expansion of the MICHELIN products into new markets.  。 It will also increase the breadth of solutions NFA offers.”

“Such cooperation will bring additional value to our customers that require innovative, affordable, agile and technologically advanced while more “life-style” products.” Olive Han, Regional Manager of Michelin Lifestyle said, "Adding NFA's extensive service retail chains to our capabilities makes good sense and we believe that the two companies complement each other's strengths. We are excited to leverage on NFA’s strength in both manufacturing and retail to roll out MICHELIN products to more consumers.”

About NFA
New Focus Auto is the leading automobile after-sales service provider in Greater China.  It currently operates more than 100+ direct-own outlets throughout Greater China region, providing basic automobile maintenance, car wash, professional detailing, modifications, body repairs and the sales of auto accessory products.   In addition to direct-own stores, New Focus also manufactures, wholesales & distributes various auto accessory products throughout Greater China and other North America & European markets. 

About New Focus Lighting & Power Technology (Shanghai) Co.,Ltd
Founded in 1989, NFA’s manufacturing unit was once the major profit generator - focusing on automotive electronics and power-related parts & accessories; over 80% of its sales is for export, mainly North America, to customers such as Canadian Tires, Costco, Autozone and Walmart…etc. NFA has de-emphasized its manufacturing business in recent years and has transformed from a car parts manufacturer to a leading auto parts distributor as well as one of the largest after-sales service retail chains operator in Greater China.

ABOUT MICHELIN
Dedicated to the improvement of sustainable mobility, Michelin designs, manufactures and sells tires for every type of vehicle, including airplanes, automobiles, bicycles, earthmovers, farm equipment, heavy-duty trucks and motorcycles. Michelin employs 115,000 people, has sales organizations in more than 170 countries and operates 69 production sites in 18 countries throughout five continents. The company also publishes travel guides, hotel and restaurant guides, maps and road atlases and offers electronic mobility support services, on ViaMichelin.com. Research and innovation development is being taken care of in technology centres in Asia, Europe and North America.

About Michelin LifestyleMichelin Lifestyle Ltd. is a subsidiary of the Michelin Group charged with the management of its brand extension program using the licensing model.  Michelin Lifestyle was established in 2000 to increase the recognition of MICHELIN brand and to develop closer relationships with consumers In 2011, Michelin Lifestyle operations represented more than 75 licensing partnerships worldwide; sales in over 85 countries and through more than 30,000 outlets, including the leading automotive accessory chains.

NFA Opens New Plant

NFA WAS NFA celebrates its foundation of the new workshop

September 7th,2007, a most beautiful autumn season and a splendid day, which implies a harvest future, we, New Focus Lighting & Power Technology (Shanghai) CO.,LTD held a grand opening ceremony in the new plant to celebrate the foundation of the new workshop.
 
The new plant locates in the east Wai Qing Song Road in Qing Pu district, covers an area of 79,184 square meters. The 1st construction area spends total 60 million with 31,000 square meters. The whole project will take around 8 months to complete.
 
The cutting ribbon ceremony is extremely prosperous. Mr Hong Weibi, CEO of the company, attended the opening ceremony and delivered an opening speech. Vice president, board of director, Mr Wu Guanhong and other leaders of NFA company as well as the representatives from the construction company & Supervision company all participated in the ceremony.
 
The upcoming project is not only the most important manufacture project of New Focus, but also a big event of the group company. It also marks a new milestone of New Focus manufacturing part, will absolutely speed up to increase the production capacity and improve quality of the manufacture.
 
Thousands of New Focus staff will take this important moment of the construction of the new factory, down-to earth, open up and bring forth new ideas, be up-and-coming, create a bright future for New Focus with their wits and diligence.
