1994   Shanghai's New Focus Auto Parts Co., Ltd. (NFA) was founded in Shanghai Qing Pu Industrial Park with a registered capital of 1.1 million dollars by Mr. Weibi Hung.

1999   First batch of NFA inverters was exported.
2000   NFA set up a second factory with a total area of 12328 ㎡among which 7000 ㎡is for manufacturing.

2001   NFA established "New Focus (Shanghai) Lighting and Power Technology Co., Ltd (NFLP) with a registered fund of 2.4 million dollars and a general investment of 4.8 million dollars.

2001   NFA was awarded the "High-Tech's Enterprise” by Shanghai's Scientific and Technological Committee for our innovative achievements in science and technology.

2002   NFA set up Shanghai's New Focus Auto Repairing & Servicing Co., Ltd (New Focus Autolife) and quickly began to expand into domestic Chinese automotive aftermarket.

2005   The Group Company’s stock was successfully listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange with the stock number HK0360, This is the first time for an automotive aftermarket retailer in mainland China to be listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

2005   New Focus Autolife successfully merged with Shanghai Li Keliang Auto Maintain Service Limited Company and Hualiang Business Training School. The chained franchise now increased to 66 stores.

2006   “New Focus Autolife” auto lifestyle shop is set up, “New Focus Autolife” merged with the largest automotive aftermarket retailer in Taiwan and set up Chengdu Autolife shop.

2007   New Focus Autolife successfully merged with the largest automotive aftermarket retailer in Beijing.

2009   NFA set up a new factory with a total area of 80000 ㎡in Shanghai Qing Pu Industrial Park
